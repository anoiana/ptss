M·ªói m·ªôt quan s√°t s·∫Ω c√≥ x√°c su·∫•t l√† \(\pi_k\mathcal{N}(\boldsymbol{\mu}_k,\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_k)\), nh∆∞ v·∫≠y ta s·∫Ω c√≥ ph∆∞∆°ng tr√¨nh likelihood l√† \[ L(\theta) = \prod_{i=1}^N\prod_{k=1}^K[\pi_k\mathcal{N}(\boldsymbol{\mu}_k,\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_k)]^{z_{ik}} \] v√† suy ra h√†m log-likelihood \[ l(\theta) = \sum_{i =1}^N\sum_{k=1}^Kz_{ik}[\ln\pi_k+\ln\mathcal{N}(\boldsymbol{\mu}_k, \boldsymbol{\Sigma}_k)] \tag{3.1} \]
