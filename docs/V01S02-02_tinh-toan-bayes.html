✑ Giới Thiệu Bayes

📅 2021-06-13

(tham khảo tại Reich and Ghosh 2019)

DRAFT

1 Gibbs sampling

Let us consider the following random samples \[ \begin{align} & Y_1, \dots, Y_n \stackrel{\text{iid}}{\sim} \mathcal{N}(\mu,\sigma^2), \quad \text{where} \\ & \mu \sim \mathcal{N}(\gamma,\tau^2), \text{ and }\quad \sigma^2 \sim \mathcal{InvGam}(a,b) \end{align} \tag{1.1} \] thus, we obtain conditional posterio distribution as follows \[ \mu|\sigma^2,\boldsymbol{Y} \sim \mathcal{N}\bigg(\frac{{\boldsymbol{Y}}^{\top}\boldsymbol{1}_n(1/\sigma^2)+\gamma/\tau^2}{n/\sigma^2+1/\tau^2},\frac{1}{n/\sigma^2+1/\tau^2}\bigg) \tag{1.2} \] \[ \sigma^2|\mu,\boldsymbol{Y} \sim \mathcal{InvGam}\bigg(n/2+a, (1/2)(\boldsymbol{Y} -\mu\boldsymbol{1}_n)^\top(\boldsymbol{Y} -\mu\boldsymbol{1}_n)+b \bigg) \]

2 Metropolis-Hastings (MH) sampling

Ta xem xét phân bố sau \[ Y_i|\boldsymbol{\beta }\sim \mathcal{Poi}[N\exp(\beta_1+\beta_2i)] \] với \(i = 1,\dots,4\), như vậy ta có hàm likelihood \[ f(\boldsymbol{Y}|\boldsymbol{\beta}) \propto \prod_{i=1}^4\exp\{-N\exp(\beta_1+\beta_2i)\}\exp\{Y_i(\beta_1+\beta_2i)\} \]
